# AVALON STORES APPLICATION under MS ACCESS Requires ACE Microsoft Access Database Engine 2016 Redistributable to be installed on to the client computer - Check for 32 or 64 bit version

Functions - include Issuing parts to a job Receipting of parts into stock with a release note number\ Returning parts back into stock - with log Reporting of \ Stock Value\ Issues to a Job\ Expired shelflife on items\ Parts that are at minimum stock level\

  • Receipting of parts with a release note as required by the following CAA rules in New Zealand
  • https://www.aviation.govt.nz/rules/
  • Definitions: 19.1 Release Note
  • Supply control procedures: 19.321
  • Records 19.323: (b)(4), (b)(5) and (b)(6)
  • Maintenance Records 43.69 (a)(2) and (f)
    • \
  • AC00-2 Storage and Distribution of Aeronautical Supplies – Rotation of Issue and Storage Limiting Periodli>.\
  • AC00-2 Storage and Distribution of Aeronautical Supplies – A Typical Stores System
